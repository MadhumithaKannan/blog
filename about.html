About me

This is what I do.

Hello!

I'm Madhumitha Kannan, an undergraduate majoring in Computer Science and Engineering based in Chennai, India.

Two years ago, never for once did I imagine myself to study or work in technology because I didn't know anything about it. In a weird bit of happenstance, I ended up pursuing a career in it. I had to work hard to learn and practice coding so I could catch up with my classmates. I spent the last two years learning new programming languages, web development and data science, mostly through blog posts and online tutorials. And that is what motivated me to start my blog.

This blog is a place where I will share my knowledge in the form of tutorials and my experience as a young developer with you. I will try to keep my content simple and beginner-friendly so that my readers find it easy and feel confident to learn the complexities of software and technology. I hope my content inspires you and helps you become a better and more efficient programmer.
